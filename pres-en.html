The Iberian exception

The IBerian EXception (IBEX)

Begins on June 15, 2022

Decouples the price of electricity from int'l gas prices:

  1. Sets limit to the price of gas-powered electricity
  2. Adjustment mechanism covers for potential loses
  3. Paid by consumers + exports to France

The exception of Iberia

    Strategies against energy crisis accros EU:

    • Tax reductions
    • Direct transfers
    • Retail market regulation

    IBEX: only measure to intervene wholesale market

Experimental setup

    The IBEX presents a natural experiment

    • While the Iberian market operates under new rules,
    • Other EU countries continue to follow directives

    Research goal:

      Estimating the causal impact of the IBEX on prices

Discussion

What explains the difference?

    Spain

    • PVPC is indexed by day-ahead price
    • Serves ~40% of households and firms
    • Direct link between DAP and NRG

    Portugal

    • Retailers favor longer-term contracts
    • Slower link between DAP and NRG

The IBEX is responsible for...

    -40% in wholesale electricity price

    Spain: immediate and strong effect on CPI

    • -19 pts in energy inflation
    • -3.5 pts in overall inflation

    Portugal: Effect with 6-month delay

    • -12 pts in energy inflation in 2023
    • No effect on overall inflation
